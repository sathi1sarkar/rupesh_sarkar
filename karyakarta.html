Restoring Lives After Political Violence

I’m committed to helping families who have suffered from post-poll violence. Through fundraising efforts, we aim to address both their physical and emotional scars, including the challenges faced by neo-refugees. We provide safe shelters, ensuring these families have a secure place to start anew.

Additionally, we help them navigate the process of securing financial and governmental support, empowering them to rebuild their lives. Our ongoing Empowerment Program is a beacon of hope, reminding us that, together, we can create a brighter future for those in need.
